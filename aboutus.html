Humble Beginnings


Who are We?

Since its founding in 2019, O2 Homes has strived to create an environment for all to receive health and energy.

Founded in Jackson, Wyoming O2 Homes is a group of dedicated engineers and scientists who are dedicated to bringing high pressured and pure oxygen to homes

O2 Homes services people throughout the rocky mountains who are deprived of the health oxygen can provide in order to create a better world
