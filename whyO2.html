Why O2?

O2 Homes: Health for the Future

~Many people today suffer from oxygen deficiency, depriving themsevles of many of the joys of life~

O2, the bodies fuel:

  • Oxygen is the driving force behind a significant portion of our bodies functions, it drives healing, energy production, and growth in the brain
  • A lack of Oxygen can result in low energy, aches and pains in the body, and deficient brain functions
  • Sufficient oxygen with lacking pressure can also result in the above symptoms. Pressure is what pushes oxygen into our blood stream giving us its desired effects

More O2, More Life:

  • Increasing oxygen in our bodies increases healing, happiness and performance
  • Increasing the pressure in our environment allows our bodies to absorb more of the oxygen we receive
  • O2 Homes delivers both of those things to improve health in all aspects
